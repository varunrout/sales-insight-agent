# Product Strategy Brief

## Product portfolio

Enterprise Suite products have lower unit volume but materially higher average order value. Insight Enterprise Cloud and Revenue Intelligence Pro should remain the anchor offers for enterprise accounts because they create larger orders, stronger retention potential and better expansion paths.

Analytics Platform products sit in the middle of the portfolio. Pipeline Analytics and Forecast Studio generate broad demand across Enterprise and Mid-Market segments and are effective entry points for customers that are not ready for a full enterprise suite commitment. They are also useful attach products for Direct sellers because they preserve a healthier margin profile than promotion-led Marketplace sales.

Automation Tools produce higher unit volume at a lower average order value. CRM Workflow Pack and Lead Scoring Add-on are useful for new customer acquisition, particularly in SMB and Mid-Market segments. The strategy should be to use them as land products, then move qualified customers toward Analytics Platform or Enterprise Suite bundles.

Services revenue is lower volume and operationally intensive, but it supports adoption of the higher-value products. Implementation Services and Enablement Workshop should be packaged with enterprise deployments when they improve activation, renewal confidence or expansion potential.

## Margin strategy

Direct channel gross margin is the strongest in the dataset. Sales motions that can remain Direct-led should be prioritized for Enterprise Suite and Analytics Platform opportunities. Partner-led deals need clearer rules for discount approval, especially in EMEA during Q3, where lower conversion and higher discounts appear together.

Marketplace can create attractive demand spikes during promotion periods, but those spikes come with lower gross margin. Marketplace promotions should focus on Automation Tools and selected Analytics Platform offers where acquisition value justifies discounting. Enterprise Suite discounting should remain limited because price cuts can reduce revenue quality without creating enough additional volume.

APAC needs a deliberate margin improvement plan. The region is growing fastest, but lower margin means the business should monitor discount depth, product mix and support costs. The best path is not to slow APAC growth, but to move a larger share of demand toward Direct and Analytics Platform bundles as the market matures.

## Product actions

Maintain Enterprise Suite as the premium revenue anchor. Use Automation Tools for efficient acquisition, then build upgrade paths into Analytics Platform and Enterprise Suite. Keep Services attached to complex deployments where it improves long-term account value. Review Partner discounting ahead of Q3 and reserve Marketplace promotions for products where volume sensitivity is expected to offset margin dilution.
